Transformational executive coaching
\nfor your business<\/h1>\n
Undertaking transformational coaching<\/strong> means giving yourself a challenge in order to discover\u00a0and give\u00a0value to your uniqueness. \u00a0 It \u00a0means being ready to work on your potential as well as on your\u00a0vulnerabilities, to become more aware of your way of being, accepting it without fear.<\/h5>\n

Stepping aside from the usual context and entering a safe\u00a0and contemplative space, the coachee is allowed to observe real life from a new point of view and to identify new possible behaviors and solutions.<\/p>\n

While helping the coachee achieve progressive goals, transformational coaching takes him\/her along a process that aims to increase his\/her leadership and personal impact, to give value to his\/her predispositions, to realize more and more how the personal story has shaped his\/her way of thinking and behaving.<\/p>\n

This path provides the necessary tools to recognize strengths and \u201cblind spots\u201d, to understand their impact on relationships and to face the leadership\u2019s challenge<\/p>\n

\u201cThis coaching path is useful for opening\u00a0new possible options of behaviors\u201d<\/em><\/p><\/blockquote>\n

It\u2019s a “focused oriented” process built through confidence, listening, proximity\u00a0to\u00a0emotions, new questions and reflection on the impact we want to have at work and in our life.<\/p>\n

Andrea Vitullo is specialized in transformational coaching paths for executives. He is a leadership development consultant with Board management experience across a wide range of industries and organizational levels.<\/h5>\n

For 15 years<\/strong>, Andrea has coached top managers supporting them in developing authentic relational skills, becoming more effective in enabling the power of different multicultural teams, leveraging people diversity and favoring inclusive policies inside corporations. Andrea supports managers in using their expertise, motivations and desires to promote impact and results in organizations.<\/p>\n

Coaching specialties: leader as a coach, emotional intelligence, presence and focus, team development, cross-cultural and diversity issues, mindfulness and presence.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n
